Weekly Exchange Stats

  • Analyzing transactions to and from exchanges can give a good indication of the supply and demand of a crypto asset.
  • An increase in outflow of a crypto asset from exchanges corresponds to decreased supply.
  • An increase in inflow of stablecoins to exchanges corresponds to increased demand.

Bitcoin
  • Between Oct 9 to Oct 11 the average inflow was ~$205M & average outflow was ~$221M. Comparing to Oct 12 till Oct 14, there was a -50% change in both inflow and outflow.
  • On Oct 15 there was an increase in net flow of ~$22M while the price of BTC fell sharply on the same day.

Ethereum
  • Large ETH inflow of  ~$38M occurred on Oct 09. Since then, low positive net flow volumes on the rest of the days signal that traders are selling and buying ETH in similar quantities.
  • Similar to BTC prices, between Oct 09 to Oct 12, ETH prices have similar trends despite very different net flows.

Stablecoins
  • We observe a significant drop in Stablecoin outflow on Oct 12 to Oct 13, caused by the weekend period.

Overview
  • Outflow amounts have increased with BTC & ETH outflows up by 4% and 9% respectively.
  • Similarly, USDT Omni outflows are up by 56% indicating a conservative trading atmosphere.
