Weekly Exchange Stats

  • Analyzing transactions to and from exchanges can give a good indication of the supply and demand of a crypto asset.
  • An increase in outflow of a crypto asset from exchanges corresponds to decreased supply.
  • An increase in inflow of stablecoins to exchanges corresponds to increased demand.

Bitcoin
  • After an 8% drop in price at the start of the week, bitcoin rallied strongly reaching a 14 day-high.
  • The price drop on 28th August coincided with a BTC net flow of $28M.
  • Between the 29th Aug and 2nd Sept, whilst the price was low there was a net outflow from exchanges of $57M.

Ethereum
  • The ETH price closely followed the BTC price, with a sharp price drop on the 28th Aug and price hike on the 2nd Sept for both assets.
  • Whilst BTC net flows were largely negative after the price drop, ETH was positive during this period.

Stablecoins
  • Aggregated net flows for stablecoins were negative throughout the week.
  • Total stablecoin on-chain exchange flows increased 3x as BTC price surged on Sept 2nd.
  • $51M worth of stablecoins left exchanges on 3rd Sept.

Overview
  • BTC exchange outflows show a 19% week on week increase.
  • ETH exchange flows show a week on week decline for the second time in a row; inflow down by 12%
  • Stablecoins show a large increase in exchange flows across all assets, with 17% increase in inflow and 54% increase in outflow versus the previous week.
