Weekly Exchange Stats

  • Analyzing transactions to and from exchanges can give a good indication of the supply and demand of a crypto asset.
  • An increase in outflow of a crypto asset from exchanges corresponds to decreased supply.
  • An increase in inflow of stablecoins to exchanges corresponds to increased demand.

Bitcoin
  • Last week we foreshadowed the price increase of BTC, and this week the price went up by 29%.
  • Simultaneously, the exchange flow volume increased throughout the week, except during weekends (3rd and 4th August).

Ethereum
  • ETH price increased by only 13% this week, less than half of BTC's price hike.
  • In the past two days, exchanges were flooded by ETH worth 60M USD, increasing the supply side of ETH.

Stablecoins
  • On average, outflows were higher than inflows by 25%

Overview
  • The trends in exchange flows have reversed compared to last week.
    • BTC inflows from -24% last week to +46% this week
    • BTC outflows from -12% last week to +17% this week
    • ETH inflow from -31% last week to +25% this week
    • ETH outflows from -39% last week to +15% this week
