Weekly Exchange Stats

  • Analyzing transactions to and from exchanges can give a good indication of the supply and demand of a crypto asset.
  • An increase in outflow of a crypto asset from exchanges corresponds to decreased supply.
  • An increase in inflow of stablecoins to exchanges corresponds to increased demand.

Bitcoin
  • On 27th July, the BTC price dropped $600 USD within one hour.
  • In the following three days, Bitcoin worth $248M USD left the exchanges (i.e. outflow minus inflow), with the outflow outpacing the inflow by 85%.
  • Following the decrease in the supply of BTC, the price shows a positive trend.

Ethereum
  • ETH dropped by 6% at the same time as the BTC price drop.
  • Unlike BTC, after the price drop, ETH worth $27M USD was moved *into* exchanges, with the inflow outperforming the outflow by 56%.

Stablecoins
  • During the three days until 27th July, stablecoins worth $60M USD left the exchanges, showing little demand for buying crypto assets.
  • During the three days after 27th July, stablecoins worth $31M were moved into exchanges, indicating a growth in demand for cryptocurrency.

Overview
  • Exchange flows decreased across the board compared to last week 
  • However, the rate of this reduction has reduced.
    • BTC inflows from -40% last week to -24% this week
    • BTC outflows from -34% last week to -12% this week
