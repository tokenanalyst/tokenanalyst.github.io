Weekly Exchange Stats

  • Analyzing transactions to and from exchanges can give a good indication of the supply and demand of a crypto asset.
  • An increase in outflow of a crypto asset from exchanges corresponds to decreased supply.
  • An increase in inflow of stablecoins to exchanges corresponds to increased demand.

Overview
  • We observe significant increases in trading activity ETH this week. Likely caused by ETH price increasing by ~18% between Feb 12 to Feb 18.
  • The inflow and outflow average transaction values are up by 41% and 54%, respectively, suggesting that bigger players are trading during volatile market conditions.
  • A total of ~$195M worth of BTC and ~$30M of ETH went into exchanges this week.
