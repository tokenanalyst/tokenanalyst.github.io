Weekly Exchange Stats

  • Analyzing transactions to and from exchanges can give a good indication of the supply and demand of a crypto asset.
  • An increase in outflow of a crypto asset from exchanges corresponds to decreased supply.
  • An increase in inflow of stablecoins to exchanges corresponds to increased demand.

Overview
  • We observe decreased trading activity this week for BTC, ETH and the majority of stablecoins. Likely caused by prices decreasing by 8% and 11% between Feb 21 to Feb 27 for BTC and ETH, respectively.
  • A total of ~$63M of BTC and ~$98M of USDT ERC20 went out of exchanges this week as prices decreased.
  • This week, ~$300M worth of USDT ERC20 went into Binance as Tether was performing a chain swap from Tron to USDT ERC20.
