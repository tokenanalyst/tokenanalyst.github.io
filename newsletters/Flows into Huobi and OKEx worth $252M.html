 Correlating BSV price swings with BTC inflows 
As with everyone else in crypto, we were watching the BSV price spike over the past few days closely.
  • Our alerting service picked up significant BTC inflows into Huobi and OKEX, which were two of the exchanges where a good portion of the BSV trading volume occurred.
  • As you can see in the hourly charts above, in the hours preceding the BSV price spikes on the 14th, both exchanges see an uptick in BTC inflows as traders looked to capitalize on and further fan the flames of the BSV uptick.

Weekly Exchange Stats

  • Analyzing transactions to and from exchanges can give a good indication of the supply and demand of a crypto asset.
  • An increase in outflow of a crypto asset from exchanges corresponds to decreased supply.
  • An increase in inflow of stablecoins to exchanges corresponds to increased demand.

Overview
  • We observe a significant increase in the inflow amount of BTC and ETH by 16% and 23% respectively, as their prices further increased by 6% and 15% from Jan 08 to Jan 14.
  • Interestingly, the amount and average transaction value of ETH outflow are up by 47% and 38% suggesting bigger players are buying during this price upswing.
