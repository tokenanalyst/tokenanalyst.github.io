Weekly Exchange Stats

  • Analyzing transactions to and from exchanges can give a good indication of the supply and demand of a crypto asset.
  • An increase in outflow of a crypto asset from exchanges corresponds to decreased supply.
  • An increase in inflow of stablecoins to exchanges corresponds to increased demand.

Overview
  • We observe that all native tokens and stablecoins amounts and number of transactions for inflows and outflows are down this week; as there was large trading activity the previous week caused by a 12% BTC price decrease the previous week.
  • A total of $91M worth of BTC went out of exchanges and $13M worth of ETH went into exchanges this week, signaling that traders are buying BTC & selling ETH.
