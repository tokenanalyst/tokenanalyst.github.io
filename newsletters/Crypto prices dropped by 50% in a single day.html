Weekly Exchange Stats

  • Analyzing transactions to and from exchanges can give a good indication of the supply and demand of a crypto asset.
  • An increase in outflow of a crypto asset from exchanges corresponds to decreased supply.
  • An increase in inflow of stablecoins to exchanges corresponds to increased demand.

Overview
  • Last week was the most volatile week with BTC and ETH prices dropping by 50% on a single day (12th March 2020)
  • Increased volatility led to increased exchange flows, average daily inflow amounts are up by 77% for BTC, up by 50% for USDT, whereas 35% for ETH. 
  • A total of $96M worth of BTC, $138M worth of ETH and $260M went into exchanges. All these assets are still sitting on exchanges waiting for the perfect time to buy.
