Weekly Exchange Stats

  • Analyzing transactions to and from exchanges can give a good indication of the supply and demand of a crypto asset.
  • An increase in outflow of a crypto asset from exchanges corresponds to decreased supply.
  • An increase in inflow of stablecoins to exchanges corresponds to increased demand.

Overview
  • We observe that BTC prices have decreased by 12% this week, while the average weekly BTC inflows to exchanges increased by 45% and average weekly outflows to exchanges increased by 50% this week.
  • A total of $114M worth of BTC and $19M worth of ETH went into exchanges this week, signaling that traders are selling their BTC & ETH.
  • While the majority of stablecoins have increased in inflows and outflows, USDT Omni and DAI have decreased this week in on-chain volume and number of transactions.
